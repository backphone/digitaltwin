v60Ka 2
65cm New Ka-band Maritime VSAT Antenna System
The v60Ka 2 is a lightweight Ka-band stabilized VSAT maritime antenna system with a single cable solution to reduce installation time and costs. The v60Ka 2 is ready for service on Telenor’s high throughput Thor-7 network and ensures outstanding performance and reliability for smaller vessels navigating even the most challenging ocean conditions. The v60Ka 2 is easily upgradeable to a high power BUC or transceiver system enabling higher throughput.
