GX60NX 2/HP
New 65cm Inmarsat GX Fleet Xpress Terminal
Intellian GX60NX 2 is a 2.5GHz Ka- Wideband ready Global Xpress terminal with a 65cm diameter reflector. The GX60NX 2 is designed with a compact lightweight antenna enabling easy and convenient installation. The GX60NX 2 has a transceiver system and GX60NX features a BUC. The GX60NX 2 HP incorporates an upgraded 9W high-powered transceiver.
The All-In-One GX BDT integrates the GX Modem and GX Mediator, making it simple and easy to install in an FX rack. The GX60NX 2’s new operation platform ensures quick configuration and activation with its smart operational software AptusNX.
