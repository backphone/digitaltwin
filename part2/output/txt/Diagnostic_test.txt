
1. Press Menu (M) key on ACU front panel;
2. Press LEFT key to move cursor to YES and press OK key to enter setup mode.
3. Select "ANTENNA" menu, press OK key
4. Press arrow keys to look for "DIAGONOSIS" (it's not on display initially) and press OK key to enter DIAGONOSIS menu
5. Press DOWN arrow key to select "FULL TEST" option and press OK key to execute it

Antenna will then go through a sequence of 15 tests. You will see on ACU display:

Solid Dot: means test passed
-: means test is not applicable and skipped
? : means under progress
Numbers: means a particular test has failed. For example, 2 means test number 2 failed

Please wait for all tests to complete, and let us know the outcome of diagnostic test.
