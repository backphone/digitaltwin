Change/check IP address of your PC:

1)Click the Start button (in left down corner of the screen)
2)In the search box, type network connections, then open it
3)Right-click the connection we need to change, most frequently called Local Area Connection and then click Properties. 

4)Click the Networking tab, click on Internet Protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4) and then click Properties. Click Use the following IP address, and then:
-	in the IP address line, type:  192.168.55.10
-	in the Subnet mask line, type: 255.255.255.0
-	leave it blank in default gateway line and DNS

5)Click OK button and next Close button.
